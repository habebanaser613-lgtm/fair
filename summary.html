book summaries

The Cruel Prince

The Cruel Prince (by Holly Black): A dark, political fantasy following Jude, a human girl living in the treacherous world of Faerie. To find her place among the immortal and disdainful folk, she must outsmart the wicked Prince Cardan and navigate a web of betrayal and power struggles.

The Seven Year Slip

The Seven Year Slip (by Ashley Poston): A magical romance centered on an apartment that exists seven years in the past. When Clementine meets a charming chef living in her apartment's past, she must navigate a long-distance relationship across time, exploring themes of grief, timing, and finding oneself.

The Midnight Library

The Midnight Library (by Matt Haig): A philosophical novel about Nora, who finds herself in a library between life and death. Each book allows her to try a version of her life she could have lived, ultimately teaching a powerful lesson about overcoming regret and finding value in one’s current existence

The Reappearance of Rachel Price

The Reappearance of Rachel Price (by Holly Jackson): Years after Rachel Price vanished, she suddenly reappears while her family is filming a documentary about her disappearance. Her daughter, Bel, is skeptical of her mother's story and begins a tense investigation to uncover the truth of where she’s been.

Ancient Wisdom

Ancient Wisdom: Typically referring to collections of teachings from Stoic, Eastern, or Greek philosophers, these works focus on timeless principles like mindfulness, virtue, and resilience to help modern readers lead more meaningful and balanced lives.

The Silk Roads

The Silk Roads: A New History of the World (by Peter Frankopan): A major re-evaluation of world history that shifts the focus away from the West and toward the East. It explores how the trade routes of Central Asia shaped empires, religions, and the global economy from ancient times to the present day.

Clean Code

Clean Code (by Robert C. Martin): A fundamental guide for software developers that outlines the principles and best practices for writing high-quality code. It emphasizes that code should be written for humans to understand, focusing on clarity, simplicity, and maintainability.
